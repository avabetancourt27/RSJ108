How to Balance Life and College as a Journalism Student

Assembly time: 30 minutes

Effectiveness: 100%

Balancing life and college as a journalism student can feel overwhelming, but with the right tools and habits, it becomes manageable and even enjoyable.

Things You'll Need

  • A class schedule
  • Sticky notes
  • A pen
  • A calendar

1. Organize your weekly schedule. Write down all your classes, deadlines, and personal commitments so you can clearly see your workload.

2. Prioritize your assignments. Journalism often requires time-sensitive work, so plan ahead and meet deadlines early.

3. Make time for yourself. Balance school with social life, rest, and activities you enjoy.
